Speaker: Juan Pablo Aguilera

Title

The kappa-Turing degrees.

Abstract

We study a generalization of the recursively enumerable sets defined in terms of \Sigma_1-definable functionals at admissible levels of Gödel’s constructively hierarchy. These sets and the corresponding notion of reducibility give rise to a new kind of degree structure for subsets of non-projectible admissible ordinals. This is joint work with N. Greenberg and E. Hammatt.
