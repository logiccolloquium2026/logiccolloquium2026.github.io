Special Session: Proof Theory/Model Theory



Speaker: Lev Beklemishev

Title

Automatic structures and the problem of natural well-orderings

Abstract

We explore the idea of using automatic and other very efficient presentations of structures to deal with the conceptual problem of natural proof-theoretic ordinal notations. We conclude that this approach still does not meet the goals. Joint work with Fedor N. Pakhomov
