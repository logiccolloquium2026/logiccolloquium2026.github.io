Conference registration

We intend to open conference registration by the middle of March.

Expected registration fees will be in the region of £300.
There will be a slight fee reduction for ASL/BLC members, and a separate student fee.

Accommodation

We will have plenty of on-campus rooms available, and will be sold on a first-come first-serve basis. Price: £77 per night.
