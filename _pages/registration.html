There will be the option to book on-campus accommodation as an add-on to the registration (scroll down when choosing your ticket). The price is £77 per night for an ensuite room. There are plenty of rooms available, but we recommend booking early to secure your place.

To book on-campus accomodation, please book by the 24th April, 2026 (Anywhere on Earth).
